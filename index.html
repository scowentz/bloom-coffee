Our Story

Rooted in Passion,
Crafted with Care

Bloom Coffee Roasters was born from a simple conviction: that extraordinary coffee is the result of an unbroken chain of care — from the farmer who tends the trees, to the roaster who coaxes out each bean's hidden complexity.

Every lot we purchase is chosen by hand — paying well above (at least 50% above) Fair Trade prices, because we believe truly great coffee starts with human dignity.

From Farm to Cup

The Art of
The Roast

Five deliberate steps. An obsessive attention to detail.

🌱 01

Sourcing

Farms are visited to select only exceptional lots from trusted growing partners.

🔬 02

Cupping

Each lot is blind-cupped by our Q-certified roasters before we commit to a purchase.

🔥 03

Roasting

Small-batch profiles refined over dozens of test roasts to honor each bean's character.

🫙 04

Resting

Coffee degasses for 24–48 hours before sealing to ensure peak flavor in every bag.

✈️ 05

Delivery

Shipped within 48 hours of roasting. From our roastery in Zionsville, Indiana to your door at its freshest.

Small-batch specialty coffee roasted with intention, sourced with care, and shipped at its freshest.
